Be Aware

How to be safe and protect yourself:
  1. Hand washing
  2. Use of Mask
  3. Avoid the 3Cs: spaces that are closed, crowded or involve close contact
  4. Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth
  5. Clean and disinfect surfaces frequently especially those which are regularly touched
